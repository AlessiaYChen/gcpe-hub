Help for Release Section

This section must be completed whenever a release is being issued.

Themes

Select all themes associated with the topic of the release. Use the following guide:

Birth, Adoption, Death, Marriage, and Divorce
e.g. Births, marriages, deaths; legal changes of name; adoptions; order certificates, copies or extracts; online birth registration; and will notices.

British Columbians and Our Governments
e.g. About our government, ministries, open government, govTogetherBC; news; geography and demographics, BC Stats; First Nation treaties and the Aboriginal peoples.

Driving and Transportation
e.g. Licensing & insurance; DriveBC, passenger air rail & water; highway construction & maintenance; B.C. Ferries.

Education and Training
e.g. Early learning, K-12, post secondary, adult learning, ways to learn; K to 12 administration; skills training; and StudentAidBC.

Employment, Business and Economic Development
e.g. Jobs in B.C.; international trade, attracting investment, B.C. exports, economic development; funding and grants.

Environmental Protection and Sustainability
e.g. Climate change; waste management; air, land & water, plants & wildlife; environmental emergencies; pesticides & pest management; and Conservation Officer Services.

Family and Social Supports
e.g. Supports for seniors, parents & youth; employment & income assistance, disability assistance; foster parenting; accessibility; and childcare.

Farming, Natural Resources and Industry
e.g. Agriculture & seafood; B.C. building code, electricity; forestry; highway construction; liquefied natural gas, mineral exploration & mining and natural gas & oil.

Health
e.g. MSP, PharmaCare, accessing health care, public health; health forms; practitioner & professional resources; and Office of the Provincial Health Officer.

Housing and Tenancy
e.g. Rental housing, strata housing, seniors housing; owning a home; social housing; buying & selling property, building or renovating and building code.

Law, Crime and Justice
e.g. B.C. laws; court services; legal information, legal assistance, legal aid; crime prevention, criminal prosecutions and victim services.

Public Safety and Emergency Services
e.g. Emergency preparedness, emergency alerts, emergency response and assistance, fire prevention, wild fires.

Sports, Recreation, Arts and Culture
e.g. Amateur sports, B.C. Games; B.C. Parks, recreation, arts, culture, historic places, museums; funding, gaming grants and event hosting.

Taxes and Tax Credits
e.g. PST; home owner grant, first time home buyers, property transfers; carbon tax, tobacco tax, natural resource taxes, insurance tax and personal income tax.

Tourism and Immigration
e.g. Traveling to B.C., studying or working in B.C.; immigrating to B.C. and HelloBC.
